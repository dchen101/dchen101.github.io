FB

Facebook is sitting on a mountain of data. A long with its Instagram Acquisition Facebook has an ocean of data thanks to the photos users post online. All the photos that we take and upload contain data such as location, time taken, cell phone model, and so on. If they are able to cross reference that data with the information in our profiles, there is no telling what kind of insight they can gain into certain demographics. Imagine being a 20 something year old jetting across the globe and snapping pictures checking into famous tourist destinations, and all of a sudden Facebook pushes a recommendation to you. This recommendation is based on another 20 something year old user who was also trekking and checking into the same destinations a week ago. Based on the demographics and travel behavior Facebook can make a recommendation based on the information provided by people who have similar tastes and behaviors. Facebook can take it a step further and recommend something that you probably would have never even considered.
