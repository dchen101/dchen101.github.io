Map Exif

Repo: https://github.com/dchen101/Exif-JS

Details:

The project was built primarily with Javascript, html, Google Maps API, and the Exif library. Everytime a photo is taken with a cellphone data tags are automatically encoded in the photo. Information such as time taken, cellphone model, and geo location are embedded. It should be noted that geo tagging must be enabled on on your phone in order for the photos to have have the geolocation exif data. Once the photos are uploaded with the HTML5 Filereader, we then use Javascript to extract the Exif GPSLatitude and GPSLongitude from the photos. The coordinates are then passed to the Google Maps API and the location of the photo is marked on the map. The idea was to allow users to see all the places they have been around the world.
