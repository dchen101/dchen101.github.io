Coming Full Circle

About a year ago I was in Taiwan tending to some family business and getting read to join Dev Bootcamp, it’s been a little over a year and things are coming full circle. My job as a web developer will require me to travel to the company HQ in Shenzhen, China, and on my way there I will be dropping in Taipei.I will be meeting with the main technical team and the CEO. I am excited and anxious about what to expect. The startup culture is something that I have always heard and read about growing up in Silicon Valley, and it’s a great opportunity to experience it first hand. This will be my first trip to China, and I am looking forward to seeing what it will like on the other side.
