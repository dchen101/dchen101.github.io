Foodmonk

Link: http://foodmonk.herokuapp.com/#!/

Repo: https://github.com/bvirk1357/foodmonk

Details:

Food Monk aims to cater and connect adventurous eaters to authentic international cuisine prepared by home cooks from around the Bay. The app was developed using the MEAN stack, Grunt/Yeoman, and the Google Maps API. Using MongoDB as the database, Express and Node.js for the backend/routing, Yeoman/Grunt for scaffolding, and Angular for asynchronus front end work, the app is able to map out the origins of dishes from around the world using the Google Maps API. Helping to connect people to the flavors of the world.
